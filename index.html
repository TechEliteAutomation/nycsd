Our Vision

NYC Singles Dance is a concept designed to redefine the way singles connect. Through DJ'd dances and curated events in a safe, vibrant environment, we offer singles a unique opportunity to meet new people every week, throughout the year. Once launched, our events will take place at premium venues across New York City, creating the perfect setting for fresh connections.

Innovative Technology

Our custom app will allow attendees to connect in real-time, with digital name tags, interest-based matching, event registration, and post-event networking tools. These features will ensure a seamless, personalized experience at every event.

Premium Experiences

Our events will be held at upscale venues across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and the tri-state area, providing an elegant, safe, and comfortable environment for singles to meet.
